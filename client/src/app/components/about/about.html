OUR STORY

Connecting Students, One Found Item at a Time

Born from a simple idea to help students reconnect with lost belongings, CampusFind has grown into a trusted campus-wide community.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, secure platform where no student has to permanently lose their belongings on campus.

Simplify lost & found processes
Build trust within campus communities
Promote honesty and responsibility

Our Vision

To become the universal standard for campus lost & found systems across universities worldwide.

Expand to 100+ campuses by 2025
Create global student connections
Foster a culture of helping others
